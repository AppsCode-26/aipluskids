Why It Matters

AI will shape the future of education and work. Early access to understanding AI should not depend on a student's background or resources.

Our Goal

Promoting Digital Equity

To promote digital equity by ensuring more students have access to safe, responsible, and meaningful AI education — regardless of their background or resources.

AI + Kids works to ensure more students have the opportunity to learn, grow, and benefit from technology. Because a child's future shouldn't be limited by what school they attend or what neighborhood they grow up in.

What We Aim to Do

Our Commitments

Four concrete goals that guide every program, resource, and partnership we build.

🌍

Expand Access for Underserved Students

Bring AI learning opportunities directly to students who have limited access to technology resources, guidance, or AI education in their schools and communities.

🛡️

Support Safe & Responsible Technology Use

Equip students with the knowledge to use AI ethically — protecting their privacy, thinking critically about outputs, and avoiding overreliance or misuse.

📉

Reduce Gaps in Access to Emerging Technology

Close the growing divide between students who understand and benefit from AI and those who are left behind — before that gap becomes permanent.

💪

Help Students Build Confidence with Technology

Give every student the understanding and hands-on experience they need to feel confident, capable, and empowered when engaging with AI tools.

Why It Matters

The Stakes Are Real

AI will continue to shape education and the future workforce. The students who understand it will have a significant advantage — and that advantage should be available to everyone.

📚
Education AI is already changing how students learn, research, and create — with or without guidance.
💼
Future Workforce The jobs of tomorrow will require AI literacy. Early understanding builds a lasting advantage.
⚖️
Fairness When access is unequal, opportunity becomes unequal. AI + Kids works to change that.
