Why It Matters

Our Impact

Digital equity in AI education is not optional; it is essential for every child's future.

Our Goal

Promoting digital equity through accessible AI education.

To promote digital equity by ensuring more students have access to safe, responsible, and meaningful AI education; regardless of their background, resources, or zip code.

What We Aim to Do

Four pillars of change

Every initiative we run is designed to move the needle on one or more of these outcomes.

Expand Access

Expand access to AI learning for underserved students who may have limited access to technology or guidance.

Support Safe Use

Support safe and responsible technology use so students engage with AI thoughtfully and critically.

Reduce the Gap

Reduce gaps in access to emerging technologies before they compound into lasting inequalities.

Build Confidence

Help students build confidence and understanding so they can grow with technology on their own terms.

Why It Matters

AI will continue to shape education and the future workforce. Early access to understanding AI should not depend on a student's background or resources.

AI + Kids works to ensure more students have the opportunity to learn, grow, and benefit from technology. Every child deserves to enter the future equipped; not left behind.
