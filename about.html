Who We Are

Mission

What AI + Kids Stands For

AI + Kids is a student-led initiative dedicated to helping children understand and use artificial intelligence in a safe, responsible, and meaningful way.

We focus on promoting digital equity by expanding access to AI education, especially for underserved students who may have limited access to technology or guidance.

Why AI + Kids Was Created

A Problem Worth Solving

Two challenges drove the creation of AI + Kids — and both still fuel everything we do.

📱

Students Are Using AI Without Guidance

AI is becoming part of everyday learning, but many students are using it without understanding how it works or how to use it responsibly. This can lead to overreliance, misinformation, and missed learning opportunities.

🚧

Access to AI Education Is Not Equal

Some students have devices, fast internet, and knowledgeable support systems. Others — especially in underserved communities — have far fewer resources. This gap in access can widen over time and impact long-term learning and career opportunities.

💡

The Solution

AI + Kids was created to help make AI education more fair, accessible, and community-focused — bridging the gap between students who have AI guidance and those who don't.

Founder Story

The Story Behind AI + Kids

👀

Noticing the Problem

I started AI + Kids after noticing how quickly students my age began using AI for school and everyday tasks — often without really understanding how it works or how to use it responsibly.

🚧

Seeing the Inequality

I also realized that access to technology is not equal. Some students have devices, internet, and support — while others, especially underserved students, may not have the same opportunities to learn about or benefit from AI.

💡

Finding a Purpose

I wanted to create something that could make a real difference in my community — not just for students like me, but especially for those with fewer resources and less access.

🚀

Starting AI + Kids

Through AI + Kids, I focus on promoting digital equity by helping students understand AI, use it responsibly, and build confidence with technology — so every child has a fair shot at the future.

Core Values

What Guides Everything We Do

🛡️

Safety First

Teach children to protect their privacy, avoid sharing personal information, and recognize unreliable or biased AI outputs. A child who understands AI's limitations is a safer, smarter user.

⚖️

Responsible Use

Encourage ethical AI use for homework, research, and projects without copying or overreliance. Real learning happens when kids engage their own minds — AI should support that, not replace it.

🌍

Digital Equity

AI education should not depend on a student's background or resources. We actively work to expand access and ensure underserved students have the same opportunities to learn and benefit from technology.
