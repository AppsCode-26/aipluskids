About Us

Who We Are

A nonprofit dedicated to helping every child understand, question, and responsibly use artificial intelligence.

Mission Statement

Our Mission

AI Plus Kids empowers K–8 students to use artificial intelligence safely, responsibly, and creatively while preserving their natural curiosity, imagination, and independent thinking.


The organization promotes a balanced partnership with technology where AI supports learning and exploration without replacing a child's own ideas, questions, and problem-solving.

Vision

Our Vision

AI Plus Kids aims to prepare children for a future where artificial intelligence is part of everyday learning, while ensuring they remain creative thinkers, curious learners, and responsible digital citizens.

Core Purpose

What We Focus On

Three pillars that guide every program, workshop, and interaction we have with students.

💬

Understand AI

Help children understand what AI is, how it processes information, and where it can go wrong — turning the mysterious into something knowable.

🎯

Use It Properly

Show students how to use AI as a supportive tool for learning and creativity — not as a shortcut that bypasses their own thinking.

🔐

Stay Safe

Equip kids with the knowledge to protect their privacy, recognize bias, and navigate AI interactions safely and confidently.

Core Values

What We Stand For

These three values shape everything we teach and create.

🛡️

1. Safety First

Teach children to protect their privacy by avoiding sharing personal information, and recognize unreliable or biased AI outputs. A child who understands AI's limitations is a safer, smarter user.

⚖️

2. Responsible Use

Encourage ethical use of AI for homework, research, and projects without copying or overreliance. Real learning happens when kids engage their own minds first.

💡

3. Creativity & Curiosity

Preserve children's natural desire to ask questions, imagine possibilities, and think independently — even when AI tools are readily available.

Founder's Words
"

Ever since the release of ChatGPT in 2020, I have always had a passion for AI. From building apps with it, to using it as a creative outlet, and pushing the limits of it, I have learned to use it as a tool. Over the years, AI adoption has grown at a fast rate in classrooms with the teachers telling us NOT to use it. However, they failed to teach us how to correctly use AI responsibly, which just enticed more kids to use it to cheat.


I believe that instead of avoiding AI, we should empower students to understand it, question it, and use it to grow. That gave me the vision to start AI Plus Kids: a nonprofit organization that would teach students K–8 how to use AI safely and responsibly.

Founder, AI Plus Kids
