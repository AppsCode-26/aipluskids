What to Expect

What Our Workshops Will Cover

A sneak peek at the topics we're designing for curious young learners.

🤖

What Is AI?

An age-appropriate deep-dive into how AI actually works — from machine learning to the chatbots students use every day.

✏️

AI & Your Homework

Learn the difference between using AI as a tutor versus letting it do your work — and why that difference matters for real learning.

🎨

Creative AI Projects

Use AI as a creative partner to write stories, design art, and build projects — while keeping your own voice front and center.

🔍

Fact-Checking AI

AI can make things up! Learn how to verify information, spot hallucinations, and think critically about AI-generated content.

🔒

Staying Safe Online

Protect your personal information, understand data privacy, and learn what not to share with AI tools.

🌍

AI & the Future

Explore how AI is changing jobs, science, art, and daily life — and what that means for the generation growing up with it.
