What We Do

AI Literacy for Every Student

We focus on expanding access to AI education and ensuring every child — regardless of background or resources — can learn to use technology safely and responsibly.

🧠

Understand AI

Kids learn what AI is, how it works, and where it makes mistakes — turning an intimidating topic into something approachable and interesting.

⚖️

Use It Responsibly

We teach ethical AI use for homework, research, and creative projects — building habits of critical thinking, not dependence.

🌍

Promote Digital Equity

Access to AI education shouldn't depend on a student's zip code or background. We work to close the gap for underserved communities.

Our Mission

A Student-Led Initiative for Every Child

AI + Kids was created after noticing how quickly students began using AI without understanding how it works or how to use it responsibly — and that access to AI tools is far from equal.

We're focused on making AI education more fair, accessible, and community-centered, starting with the students who need it most.

Why It Matters

The AI Gap Is Growing

AI is becoming part of everyday learning — but many students are using it without guidance, and access to quality AI education is unequal.

📱
Students are already using AI

Many K–8 students use AI tools daily — often without understanding how they work or the risks involved.

🚧
Access is not equal

Underserved students often have less access to devices, internet, and AI education support — widening an already unfair gap.

🤝
Community-centered solutions

We bring AI education directly to students, families, and educators in the communities that need it most.

Founder's Story
"

I started AI + Kids after noticing how quickly students my age began using AI for school and everyday tasks, often without really understanding how it works or how to use it responsibly. I also realized that access to technology is not equal — some students have devices, internet, and support, while others, especially underserved students, may not have the same opportunities.


I wanted to create something that could make a difference in my community. Through AI + Kids, I focus on promoting digital equity by helping students understand AI, use it responsibly, and build confidence with technology.

Founder, AI + Kids
