Why We Exist

The problem we're solving

AI is reshaping education — but most kids use it without understanding how it works. And access isn't equal. AI + Kids was created to close that gap.

We believe every child deserves the tools to understand, question, and shape the technology defining their future — not just consume it.

⚖️

Digital Equity

Bridging the gap so every student has access to AI education regardless of background or resources.

🛡️

Safe, Responsible Use

Teaching children to use AI thoughtfully — with privacy awareness, ethics, and critical thinking.

🌱

Community-Focused

Building partnerships with schools, libraries, and families to bring AI literacy where it's needed most.

Common questions

Everything you need to know about AI + Kids and how we approach AI education for children.

Ask Us Anything →
AI + Kids is a student-led initiative dedicated to helping children understand and use artificial intelligence in a safe, responsible, and meaningful way. We focus on digital equity by expanding access to AI education, especially for underserved students with limited access to technology or guidance.
Our programs are designed for students in grades K–8, as well as the families, educators, and community organizations that support them. No prior technical experience is needed for students or adults to participate.
Yes. AI + Kids is a nonprofit community initiative. All programs, materials, and resources are free and designed to be accessible to every student regardless of their family's financial situation or access to technology.
Absolutely not. Our programs focus on AI literacy — understanding how AI works, using it responsibly, and thinking critically about it. This is accessible to all students, not just those with a technical background.
AI + Kids is student-led and community-focused. We specifically prioritize underserved students who may lack access to AI tools, guidance, or support systems. Our approach emphasizes digital equity, responsible use, and community partnership — not just tech skills.
We welcome partnerships with schools, libraries, community centers, and education-focused organizations. Reach out via our contact form or email info@aipluskids.org to start the conversation about how we can work together.
Children already interact with AI daily — in search, recommendations, and educational tools. Understanding how AI works helps them engage with technology critically and responsibly, rather than passively. Early AI literacy is becoming as foundational as reading and math.
