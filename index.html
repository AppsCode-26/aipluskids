What We Do

AI Literacy for the Next Generation

We give kids the tools to understand, question, and use AI responsibly — so technology becomes a superpower, not a crutch.

🧠

Understand AI

Kids learn what AI actually is, how it works, and why it sometimes makes mistakes — demystifying the technology around them.

⚖️

Use It Responsibly

We teach ethical AI use for homework and projects — encouraging original thinking rather than copy-and-paste shortcuts.

🔒

Stay Safe Online

Students learn to protect their privacy, spot biased outputs, and stay safe when interacting with AI tools.

Our Mission

Empowering Kids, Not Replacing Them

AI Plus Kids believes that children who understand AI are better prepared for the future. Our mission is to ensure every K–8 student knows how to partner with AI while keeping their own voice, curiosity, and creativity front and center.

Our Core Values

🛡️
Safety First

Teach children to protect their privacy, avoid sharing personal information, and recognize unreliable AI outputs.

⚖️
Responsible Use

Encourage ethical use of AI for homework, research, and projects — without copying or overreliance.

💡
Creativity & Curiosity

Preserve children's natural desire to ask questions, imagine possibilities, and think independently.

Founder's Words
"

Ever since the release of ChatGPT, I have always had a passion for AI. From building apps with it, to using it as a creative outlet, and pushing its limits, I have learned to use it as a tool. Over the years, AI adoption has grown fast in classrooms — with teachers telling us NOT to use it. However, they failed to teach us how to correctly use AI responsibly, which just enticed more kids to use it to cheat. I believe that instead of avoiding AI, we should empower students to understand it, question it, and use it to grow. That gave me the vision to start AI Plus Kids: a nonprofit that would teach students K–8 how to use AI safely and responsibly.

Founder, AI Plus Kids
