Founder's Message

Why I started AI + Kids

I started AI + Kids, a student-led initiative inspired by my interest in artificial intelligence and my desire to make technology more accessible to everyone.

As I began learning more about AI, I realized how powerful it can be; but also how uneven access to it is. Not all students have the same opportunities to explore or understand these tools. I wanted to create something that could help bridge that gap.

AI + Kids focuses on helping students, especially those with limited access to technology, learn how to use AI safely, responsibly, and creatively. I believe AI should not replace creativity, but support it.

Why We Exist

The problem we're solving

AI is reshaping education; but most kids use it without understanding how it works. And access isn't equal. AI + Kids was created to close that gap.

We believe every child deserves the tools to understand, question, and shape the technology defining their future; not just consume it.

Digital Equity

Our Impact

What we aim to achieve

Every program we run is designed to move the needle on one or more of these outcomes for underserved students.

Expand Access

Make AI learning available to students who have limited access to technology, guidance, or resources.

Support Safe Use

Ensure students engage with AI thoughtfully and critically, with a strong foundation in ethics and safety.

Reduce the Gap

Close gaps in AI access before they become lasting educational and career inequalities.

Build Confidence

Help students develop real confidence and agency with technology; on their own terms.
