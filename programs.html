What We Offer

Our Programs

Beginner-friendly learning experiences designed to make AI education accessible, safe, and genuinely engaging for every student.

Core Programs

Four ways we help kids learn AI

Each program is designed to meet students where they are — no prior tech experience needed.

PROGRAM 01
🤖

AI Basics for Kids

Beginner-friendly sessions that introduce what AI is, how it works, and how it is used in daily life. Students explore real examples in a supportive, approachable format.

  • What is AI and how does it learn?
  • AI in everyday life examples
  • Hands-on exploration activities
PROGRAM 02
🔒

Safe & Responsible AI Use

Workshops focused on privacy, ethical use, critical thinking, and understanding AI limitations. Students learn how to think carefully about AI outputs and their consequences.

  • Online privacy and data awareness
  • Recognizing AI limitations and bias
  • Ethical decision-making frameworks
PROGRAM 03
✨

Creative AI Learning

Hands-on activities where students use AI for writing, storytelling, brainstorming, and problem-solving. Learning by creating, experimenting, and discovering together.

  • AI-assisted writing and storytelling
  • Creative brainstorming with AI tools
  • Problem-solving projects
PROGRAM 04
🏠

Community & Family Resources

Simple guides and materials for families, educators, and students to support AI learning at home and in the community — no technical background required.

  • Family-friendly take-home guides
  • Educator classroom resources
  • At-home learning activities
