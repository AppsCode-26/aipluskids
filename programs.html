Our Programs

How We Teach AI

Four beginner-friendly programs designed to help K–8 students — and their families and educators — understand and use AI safely, responsibly, and creatively.

1

AI Basics for Kids

Beginner-friendly sessions that introduce what AI is, how it works, and how it is used in daily life. No prior tech knowledge needed — just curiosity.

🎯 Grades K–8 ⏱ Beginner 👥 Students
2

Safe & Responsible AI Use

Workshops focused on privacy, ethical use, critical thinking, and understanding AI limitations. Students learn to question AI outputs and develop healthy habits around technology.

🛡️ Safety Focus 🧠 Critical Thinking 👥 Students
3

Creative AI Learning

Hands-on activities where students use AI for writing, storytelling, brainstorming, and problem-solving — always keeping their own ideas and voice at the center.

🎨 Hands-On ✍️ Creative 👥 Students
4

Community & Family Resources

Simple guides and materials for families, educators, and students to support AI learning at home and in the community — accessible to everyone, regardless of tech background.

🏠 At Home 👨‍👩‍👧 Families 📚 Educators
